Hii !! PLAYER

RULES


1.you will get 5 tries in total to guess the country.



2.dont try to leave input field empty otherwise game will not proceed.



3.flag of the randomly generated country is displayed after your first blind guess.



4.you will be provided with 4 hints in total after each guess following a blind guess.


5.you can also press + ('shift'+ '=') to get the hint without clicking the red button


6.once you have guessed the country you can again play with another randomly generated country.
